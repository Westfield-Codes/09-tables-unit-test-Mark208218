Mercury

Mass 0.0553
Density 0.984
Gravity 0.378

    any moons (neither

  • Mercury is the fastest

Venus

Mass 0.815
Density 0.951
Gravity 0.907
  • Venus is the third
    brightest object in the
    Earth's sky.
  • Venus has a runaway
    greenhouse effect. It's
    so hot that rocks melt.

Earth

Mass 1
Density 1
Gravity 1

    largest living structure
    (though climate
    change is killing them).
  • Earth's magnetic poles
    are slowly moving
    westward.

Mars

Mass 0.107
Density 0.713
Gravity 0.377
  • Mars has 37% less
    gravity than Earth, so
